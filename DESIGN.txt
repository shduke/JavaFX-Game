Game: Rooster Roaster
Genre: 2-D Top Down Scrolling Shooter

Back story: After years of consuming poultry the fowl beasts have risen up in defense!
It is up to you to re-domesticate these rebel roosters and restore the gains to the world.

Goal: Shoot all the chickens/roosters to advance to the next level. Each level gets harder
with more foes. Eventually the boss rooster will be reached and upon defeating him
you will win the game

Mechanics: Arrow keys to move, space bar to shoot, avoid the rooster poop and chicken eggs.

Each of the ordinary levels will be composed of small chickens and roosters that have simple
attacks and movements. The boss will be much stronger, look different, and have different attacks.
